Who am I?


I’m Lance, currently an incoming 3rd year BS Computer Science student in UP Diliman. I started coding around 2 years ago, and in hindsight, I never would’ve thought I’d end up actually taking this course. I didn’t even know what it was until the college exam results came out. I was just too excited to find out that I passed UP, and I really wanted to get in no matter what course I ended up with. Safe to say, this has been a really good gamble for me.

To be honest, I haven't done much in my life yet. I'm hopeful that I'll become very successful one day, but for now, I'm willing to let my opportunities come to me and enjoy the life I have right now while its still fun.

I love watching videos. It doesn't matter if its about the most random thing ever; if its entertaining, then it has my time. I used to have dreams of being a movie director some day, but I realize that maybe that isn't really my thing, and that I'm more of just a fan of watching things.

Every now and then, I'd try to experiment with some music. It started when I used to do a lot of instrumental covers back in High School. That was when I sort of realized that I could start making my own things. It never became anything serious. I mostly just do it because its fun.

As for my plans for the future, I really don't know. What I do know is that whatever comes my way, I think I'll be ready for it. If I'm not, then I'll sure as hell be pushing through anyway.
